29/// Origin marker tool. Two thin lines are arranged to form axes indicators
30/// on the screen at the location of the origin of the coordinate system. The
31/// intersection of the lines indicates the origin and the direction of the
32/// lines from the origin indicates the positive direction along the axis.
33///
